Haiku Workshop

What can be a better way of immersing oneself in nature than discovering the wonderful world of haikus that have, over the years, bloomed in the silent blushing of flowers and the caress of a silent breeze floating through the grass? For a while, let us sit together, breathe and dive deep into the magic that three lines of poetry can create.

This workshop shall involve learning, exploration and writing in equal measure. The first part shall be a short introduction into the world of haikus. This shall be followed by a set of hands-on exercises where we shall explore the concepts of observation and imagination and craft our own haikus.
